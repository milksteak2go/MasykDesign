Case Study

NAIT Hokanson Chef in Residence: Celebrating Indigenous Cuisine

Know Your Chef, Know Your Ingredients: Promoting Culinary Excellence and Indigenous Heritage

Services

Digital Invitation Design, Branding, Social Media

Client

NAIT

Software

Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, After Effects

Challenge

To create visually compelling and informative promotional materials for NAIT’s 2025 Hokanson Chef in Residence program, featuring Indigenous chef Quentin Glabus and his unique focus on pre-colonial cuisine. The challenge was to respectfully represent Indigenous culture while adhering to NAIT’s branding and highlighting the culinary aspect of the event.

GOAL

To design eye-catching and informative promotional materials, including posters and digital invitations, that celebrate Indigenous culinary heritage, promote the Chef in Residence program, and encourage attendance at the special events with Chef Glabus.

Solution

The design generated excitement for the event, promote NAIT’s Chef in Residence program, and celebrate Indigenous culinary heritage in a respectful and engaging way.
