Case Study

Streamlining KYC for a Globalized World.

Know Your Business, Anywhere: Streamline Global Onboarding with Trulioo.

Services

Website, UX/UI Design,Performance and Growth

Client

Trulioo

Software

Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator

Challenge

Businesses struggle with streamlining Know Your Business (KYB) processes and ensuring compliance when onboarding partners globally.

Trulioo needed a compelling and informative product sheet to showcase their GlobalGateway Business Verification service for this target audience.

GOAL

Created a visually compelling product sheet for Trulioo, leveraging their branding and prioritizing clarity to showcase their GlobalGateway Business Verification solution. Emphasized concise language, intuitive layout, and impactful visuals to effectively communicate key features and benefits.

Solution

This visually engaging product sheet is designed to attract new customers and drive conversions for Trulioo. The clear and concise design highlights key features and benefits, motivating potential customers to learn more about Trulioo GlobalGateway Business Verification and ultimately contact Trulioo for further details.
