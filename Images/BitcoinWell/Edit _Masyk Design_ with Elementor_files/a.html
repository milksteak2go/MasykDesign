Bitcoin Well

Simplifying Bitcoin Purchases with e-Transfer

Bitcoin Well, a Canadian platform for buying and selling Bitcoin, needed a clear and engaging way to explain their e-Transfer service. They aimed to break away from traditional explanations and target a younger audience interested in a secure and convenient way to invest in Bitcoin.

Services
Art Direction, infographics, motion graphics, user experience (UX)
Client
Bitcoin Well
Year
2021
Software
After Effects, Photoshop, illustrator
Challenge

Bitcoin Well, a Canadian platform for buying and selling Bitcoin, needed a clear and engaging way to explain their e-Transfer service.

They aimed to break away from traditional explanations and target a younger audience
interested in a secure and convenient way to invest in Bitcoin.

Goal

My goal was to create a captivating motion graphics animation that would simplify the process of buying Bitcoin using Bitcoin Well’s e-Transfer service. The animation needed to be informative, visually engaging, and specifically highlight the benefits of using e-Transfer:

  • Speed and Convenience: Emphasize the ability to buy Bitcoin instantly directly through e-Transfer, eliminating the need for pre-funding an account.
  • Security: Underscore the secure nature of using Bitcoin Well’s platform for transactions and the recommendation to use a non-custodial wallet for maximum security.
Solution

I developed a dynamic motion graphics animation that visually represented the step-by-step process of using Bitcoin Well's e-Transfer service to purchase Bitcoin.

The animation leveraged clear and concise visuals and text overlays to guide viewers through each step, demonstrating how to use the e-Transfer interface.  Smooth transitions and a modern visual style kept viewers engaged and made the animation enjoyable to watch. This engaging format simplified a multi-step financial process into easily digestible steps, making it easier for viewers to understand and retain the key steps involved in using Bitcoin Well’s e-Transfer service for Bitcoin purchases.

To further enhance user understanding and engagement, I also created a comprehensive infographic that visually promoted the key benefits of using Bitcoin Well’s e-Transfer service: Cost, Speed, and Security.
