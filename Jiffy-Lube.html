Edmonton Oilers Winterization Campaign

Services

Art Direction, Design, digital ad, Strategy

Client

Jiffy Lube

Software

Photoshop, Illustrator

Challenge

Jiffy Lube, a quick-service oil change provider, faced a unique marketing hurdle in Edmonton. With winter’s harsh bite approaching, they needed to convince a notoriously hockey-obsessed city to prioritize their vehicles amidst the playoff fervor.

GOAL

The campaign aimed to tap into the enthusiasm of Edmonton Oilers fans for hockey to raise awareness about vehicle winterization. The hockey-themed campaign on the Oilers website utilized eye-catching visuals and a compelling message, emphasizing winter preparedness and offering a $10 discount on services.

Solution

The creative execution using hockey themes, the $10 discount incentive, and the strategic placement on the Oilers website likely resonated with the target audience.

This approach increased awareness of the importance of winterization and drove potential customers to Jiffy Lube locations for winterization services.

Overall, this Jiffy Lube campaign demonstrates the effectiveness of tailoring marketing messages to a specific audience and leveraging a shared passion point to promote a service.
