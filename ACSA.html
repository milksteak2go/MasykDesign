Case Study

ACSA: COR Modernization Campaign

ACSA needed to design modern and engaging visual assets, like posters and digital materials,
to effectively communicate their COR Modernization project to the construction industry.

Services

Art Direction, Poster Design, Postcard Design, Social Media Ad Design

Client

Alberta Construction Safety Association (ACSA)

Software

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign

Challenge

ACSA needed a visually compelling way to communicate the details of their COR Modernization project to a diverse audience, from corporate safety managers to field workers. The challenge was to create designs that were both informative and engaging, while also maintaining the credibility and professionalism of the ACSA brand. They wanted to move beyond traditional, corporate designs and create something modern and relatable.

GOAL

My goal was to develop a range of assets that would effectively communicate the key information about the COR Modernization project. These assets needed to:

  • Inform: Clearly explain the changes and benefits of the updated COR program.
  • Engage: Capture the attention of the target audience and encourage participation in the consultation process.
  • Modernize: Reflect a contemporary design aesthetic that resonates with a broad audience.
  • Credibility: Maintain the trusted image of the ACSA brand.
Solution

By creating a range of modern and engaging posters, postcards, and digital materials for the ACSA, I successfully enhanced communication and drove engagement for their COR Modernization project. The positive results included increased engagement on digital platforms, broader reach within the construction sector, and positive feedback reflecting improved understanding of the new program, ultimately contributing to a smoother transition and greater adoption of the modernized COR system.
