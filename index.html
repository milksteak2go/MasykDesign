ABOUT

I'm a graphic designer with a passion for crafting compelling visuals and user-friendly experiences. I thrive on bringing ideas to life across a variety of platforms, from websites and digital marketing to branding and social media content. My goal is to help businesses and organizations tell their stories effectively and achieve measurable results through impactful design.

My foundation in graphic design comes from my diploma in Graphic Communications from NAIT, complemented by six years of practical experience. I'm highly proficient in the Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator, InDesign, and Photoshop are my daily drivers), and I have a strong grasp of design principles like composition, color theory, and typography. I'm also comfortable collaborating with developers, thanks to a solid understanding of front-end development concepts. I'm always eager to learn and stay on top of the latest design trends and technologies.
