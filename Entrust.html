Case Study

Making Funding Accessible, Empowering Your Future.

Entrust is revamping its funding information webpages to be clear, accessible, and user-friendly, empowering people with disabilities to find the resources they need.

Services

Website, UX/UI Design,Performance and Growth

Client

Entrust Disability Services

Software

Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator

Challenge

Entrust Disability Services recognized a critical gap in their web presence. The complex and user-unfriendly nature of government websites containing funding information presented a barrier for potential clients seeking clarity and guidance.

Their existing funding information webpages mirrored these issues, making it difficult for users with varying tech skills and abilities to navigate and understand the programs and resources available.

GOAL

Leveraged my design expertise to create user-friendly webpages for Entrust, ensuring accessibility (WCAG compliant) and clear communication of their funding programs. The design incorporates engaging visuals and intuitive navigation to enhance the user experience

Solution

The redesigned webpages will be more useful, intuitive, and engaging, leading to increased access to Entrust's funding programs and support services.
